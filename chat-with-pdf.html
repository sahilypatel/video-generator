Currently, Chat with PDF supports PDF documents of all types including research papers, contracts, reports, manuals, and ebooks. We're actively working on expanding support to include DOCX, PPT, and Excel files in the near future.

Yes! You can upload multiple PDF documents and ask questions that span across all of them. This is perfect for comparing research papers, analyzing multiple reports, or cross-referencing information from different sources simultaneously.
